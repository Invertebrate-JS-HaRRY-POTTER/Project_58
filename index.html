My Favourite Character

Alex Rider

Alex Rider, 14 year old, is the main protagonist of "Alex Rider," a series by Anthony Horotwitz. He is recruited by MI6, the UK's intelligence service, and is sent on several adventurous missions. The characterstics I like about him are that he is brave, alert and curious.
